Welcome to The Right Step School

The Right Step School offers world-class, affordable education in Gilgit Baltistan. Established under the aegis of The WEG, it is committed to providing an efficient learning environment for your children, helping them compete and excel in the rapidly evolving environment of today. We offer quality education from kindergarten till Matriculation, in efficient and well-organised campuses. Our Mission is to To establish a network that imparts high quality education without discrimination. We aim to develop youth who are holistically groomed and empowered to meet the challenging world head-on. Employing the latest teaching techniques, our mission is to inculcate values, morals and ethics in our students as well as grooming them for a successful future.
